FAQ

Questions we
get asked a lot.

How long does shipping take? +

Air freight takes 3 to 5 working days. Sea freight takes 4 to 6 weeks depending on destination and customs clearance at both ends.

Do you handle customs clearance? +

Yes, we handle all export and import documentation. Your recipient will not need to deal with customs on their end.

Are packages insured? +

All shipments are insured against loss or damage. Additional insurance is available for high-value items on request.

Can I track my package? +

Yes. You receive a tracking number the moment your shipment is booked, and can monitor it in real-time on our track page.

What payment methods do you accept? +

We accept bank transfer, card payment, and cash for walk-in customers. Payment is confirmed before pickup.

Do you pick up from my location? +

Yes, we offer door-to-door pickup in Lagos and Abuja. For other cities, contact us and we will arrange the best option.
